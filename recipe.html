Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Its creamy texture and rich flavor make it irresistible for pasta lovers.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, in a pan, fry pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and saut√© briefly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain spaghetti and quickly toss with pancetta in the pan (off the heat).
  5. Add egg and cheese mixture, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season generously with black pepper and serve immediately.

Tips

Tip: For extra creaminess, reserve half cup of pasta water and mix it in slowly while combining the pasta with the egg mixture.
